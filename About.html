About NAARC

In an era of dynamic change in space, Aerospace is addressing a generation of complex challenges. We operate the only federally funded research and development center (FFRDC) committed exclusively to the space enterprise. Our technical experts span every discipline of space-related science and engineering

NAARC is a world-leading provider of onboard and ground systems for the civil aerospace market. The company’s Aerospace activities employ 1,000 people, who are helping to define tomorrow's air transport solutions: cleaner, quieter and more efficient aircraft designed to fly safely in ever-more crowded skies. NAARC provides systems and functions for all types of aircraft – commercial airliners, regional, business aircraft, and helicopters.
